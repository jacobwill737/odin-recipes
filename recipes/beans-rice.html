Texmex beans and rice

Description

This is my personal take on a texmex beans and rice meal that is easy to make and will keep you full.

Ingredients

  • two pounds of ground beef
  • three bell peppers
  • taco seasoning
  • two cans of black beans
  • two cans of fire roasted tomatoes
  • Shredded mexican blend cheese
    • \
  • one cup of cooked rice

Steps

  1. Dice up the bell peppers
  2. Add the diced up bell peppers and the ground beef into a frying pan and cook until the beef is browned.
  3. Drain the fat from the pan and add the remaining ingredients.
  4. Heat until warmed through and cheese is melted
  5. Season with taco seasoning to taste
