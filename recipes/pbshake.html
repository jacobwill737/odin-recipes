Peanut butter milkshake

Description

This is my personal take on one of the best milkshake flavors you can have

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Vanilla Ice cream
  • Blender
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Add three to four scoops of vanilla icre cream to a blender.
  2. Add half of a cup of peanut butter.
  3. Add enough milk to make it the consitency you like.
  4. Blend it up, pour it into a glass and enjoy
